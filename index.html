How to use this tool:

  1. Select map mode: Pinpoint for single points or Area for polygons
  2. Click on the map to place points or draw area boundaries
  3. Fill in the land use data form for the selected location/area
  4. Enable GPS tracking to show your live location on the map
  5. Upload photos of the land use if available
  6. Submit the data for administrator review
