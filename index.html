How to use this tool:

  1. Ensure GPS is active (check the status indicator).
  2. Click "Start Capture" to begin recording your current location as the starting point.
  3. Walk around the land and click "Add Point" at each corner/vertex.
  4. When done, click "End Capture" – the polygon will be closed and area calculated in acres.
  5. Location details (District, Subcounty, Parish) are auto‑filled from the polygon center.
  6. Fill in the rest of the form and submit for review.
